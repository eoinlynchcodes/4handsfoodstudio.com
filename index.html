ABOUT

4 Hands Food Studio make food products with a central focus on fermentation and regenerative agriculture.
Founded by Rose Greene and Margaux Dejardin and situated in Mullingar, Ireland.

Hailed as "the best pop-up restaurant in Ireland" by the Irish Times. 4 Hands pop-up dinners have earned an excellent reputation in a short space of time throughout Ireland.
